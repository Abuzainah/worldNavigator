Command:



Here are the list of commands :

-move {left|right}: to move clockwise or counter-clockwise
-move {forward|backward}: to move between rooms
-check : take one of these arguments
- mirror: loot key behind mirror
- painting: loot key behind painting
- chest: loot one item (gold, flashlight, painting)
- door: to check if the door is open or close
-look: to see what in front of you
-trade: to enter trade mood if these is a seller in front of you
-switchlight: to turn on or turn off the room light
-use key (key name): to open a closed chest or door
-use flashlight: turn on or turn off the flashlight
-playerstatus (this command run in any mode): to show your items, gold and direction
